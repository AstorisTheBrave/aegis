# Maintainers

Maintainers review connectors, policy packs, workflow templates, SDK changes,
fixtures, and documentation. Every ecosystem artifact needs a named maintainer,
immutable source revision, reproducible build digest, compatible platform range,
passing tests, and a lifecycle declaration.

Connector maintainers keep least-privilege scope evidence and prove that a v1
connector cannot issue provider writes. Reviewers may ask for newer fixtures,
compatibility evidence, or a deprecation plan before accepting a release.
